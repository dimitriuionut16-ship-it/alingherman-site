About

Composed and recorded during a short residency in Wanze area (Huy, Belgium) in April 2004, thanks to Philippe Roskam.

The initial performances by Mathieu Roskam (improvisation, Bb clarinet) and Alin Gherman (composition on Roland hp-3e) have been remixed on computer afterwards.

Dedication

Dedicated to Judith Enders.

Performers
