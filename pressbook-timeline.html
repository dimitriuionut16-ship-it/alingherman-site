"Presence Effect: Nameless" (after Samuel Beckett) — directed by Andrei SAUCHANKA (BY), with actor Ivan PATAPAU (BY), photography Volha SAVICH (BY) and Jonas FEHR (CH). Music by Alin GHERMAN.

The person loses his/her essence, language, name. The only thing which is left is his/her memories where he/she can wander along the ruins of his/her Babel which he/she erected inside of himself/herself one day.
"Individual in the Masses" — directed by Elena ZAIATS (BY), with Jonas FEHR, Evgeniya Romanovich, Irina Vasilkovskaya, Alesya Petrashevskaya, Dariya Ivanova, Maksim Shishko and Ivan Streltsov. Music by Alin GHERMAN, Lukas HUBER (CH) and Nicolas SCHMITZ (CH).

The person who had no experience of communication with those of the same kind discovers the existence of others who are like him/her. As he/she communicates with the alike, he/she loses his/her personal space. The boundaries of individuality become diluted.

May – June

Making of Percussion Instruments

Making of the first own percussion instruments: 2 Stone chimes, 4 Metal chimes (2 made of cans, 1 made of tuned tubes & 1 made of old & new spoons and forks), 2 Glass chimes (in 3 elements) and 3 (hard) Wood chimes (1 divided in 4 ranges, 1 mixed and 1 Monoregister). The majority of these instruments are to be used in the multi-percussion piece "Purple ritual". Almost all sets have been sampled (audio + video) in the H.K.B. studio recording. They are also available for renting by contacting the "Centre International de Percussion" of Geneva or by contacting the composer. See "Instruments" for detailed information.

* The very beginning...

Born in 1981 in Cluj-Napoca - Transylvania, Romania. At the age of 11, arrival in Belgium. In 1994 composition of small pieces on a Casio synthesizer. At that time, student at the Woluwé Saint Pierre Academy of Music in Brussels; class of Prof. Alain MANCHE for the piano. On 13 December at VILLA FRANCOIS GAY: he played for the first time in a concert his compositions. Since this first public performance many concerts with personal compositions.
