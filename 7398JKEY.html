Relations lumineuses

With lighting sequence for STRAND 520

Original Site Archive

About this composition

Relations lumineuses is a composition that integrates a programmed lighting sequence designed for the STRAND 520 lighting console (v.2.8.5).

The lighting sequence is an integral part of the performance, creating a dialogue between sound and light.

Lighting sequence download

The lighting sequence programmed for the STRAND 520 (v.2.8.5) is available as a downloadable package.
