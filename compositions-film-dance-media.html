Presence Effect — Nameless
opus —2012ca. 21′
+
▶ Excerpt (36″ of 21′20″)

About

Music for the theatre play “Presence Effect: Nameless” (after Samuel Beckett), directed by Andrei SAUCHANKA (BY), with the participation of actor Ivan PATAPAU (BY), photographies Volha SAVICH (BY) and Jonas FEHR (CH).

The person loses his/her essence, language, name. The only thing which is left is his/her memories where he/she can wander along the ruins of his/her Babel which he/she erected inside of himself/herself one day.
