About the instrument

Made by Alin Gherman at the H.K.B. workshop in Bern (Switzerland), May 2009. A unique instrument composed of 12 tubes (solid & hollowed) and 1 huge spoon, assembled on a platform of natural wood already prepared with holes for easy extension.

Quality hand-made manufacturing: all knots secured with hot glue, resonant tubes and spoon, aesthetically assembled. Tuning: microtonal and slightly polarized, not based on any classified chord.

These Metal Tubes chimes were especially assembled in order to resonate extremely long. The red-yellow plastic wires are long enough to allow the performer to easily isolate any non-desired tubes — the performer may choose which sounds and pitches to let ring freely.

The Metal Tubes Chimes were built as a continuation of the “Purple Ritual” chimes series, and were included in the instrumentation of the original soundtrack of “Ein Werktag”.
