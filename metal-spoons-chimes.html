About the instrument

Made by Alin Gherman at the H.K.B. workshop in Bern (Switzerland) in July 2009. This unique instrument is composed of 43 old and new spoons, forks and coffee spoons, spanning three registers — Low, Medium and High — arranged from left to right on a black wood platform with gold-painted sides.

Quality hand-made manufacturing: all knots secured with hot glue. The Metal Spoons Chimes were especially assembled in order to resonate very long.

The black plastic wires are long enough to allow the performer to easily isolate any non-desired spoons or forks on the wood platform — the performer may freely choose which sounds and pitches to let ring.
